Feed Item Card Concepts

Here are three different concepts for the main component of our "Coach's Feed". Each has a different feel and encourages different kinds of interaction. Review the examples for each to see how they handle various feed events.

A. The Structured Card

Feeling: Organized, clear, informational.
Pros: Very scannable, lots of room for details, clear separation of actions.
Cons: Can feel a bit formal and less "conversational".

Feeling: Personal, dynamic, friendly.

C. The Minimalist Item

Feeling: Efficient, clean, actionable.
Pros: Very scannable and compact. Feels like a powerful to-do list.
Cons: May feel less "human" and lacks visual flair for rewards or tips.
