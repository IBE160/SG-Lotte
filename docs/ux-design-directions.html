1. Minimalist Dashboard & Quick Log

**Philosophy:** Efficiency & Clarity. A clean, spacious interface prioritizing the current day's most important tasks. Emphasizes quick actions and clear visual separation.

  • Layout: Simple top navigation, focused "Today's Plan" card.
  • Visual Hierarchy: Very spacious, large text for current activity, clear separation.
  • Interaction: Inline quick-log buttons directly on cards. Customization is a dedicated action.
  • Visual Weight: Minimalist, lots of white space.

2. Card-Centric Progress

**Philosophy:** Motivation & Visual Progress. Presents information in digestible cards, visually emphasizing progress and making it easy to scan the week.

  • Layout: Grid of cards for daily plans and progress. Sidebar-like nav (illustrated by top nav).
  • Visual Hierarchy: Balanced, each card is a clear unit of info. Progress indicators prominent.
  • Interaction: Card tap expands for details/logging.
  • Visual Weight: Balanced, clear structure with moderate visual weight.

3. Guided Planner

**Philosophy:** Guidance & Ease of Use. Leads users through key steps, especially for new plan generation and adaptation, making complex processes feel simple.

  • Layout: Wizard-like flow for plan review and customization. Clear top nav.
  • Visual Hierarchy: Prominent current task, clear calls to action.
  • Interaction: Explicit "next step" buttons. AI suggestions are interactive options.
  • Visual Weight: Balanced, clear pathways and focus.

4. Data-Driven Customizer

**Philosophy:** Flexibility & Control. Focuses on data and detailed plan views, offering users powerful tools for granular customization and analysis.

  • Layout: Dashboard centered on metrics and data. Detailed lists for plans.
  • Visual Hierarchy: Denser with more data points visible, organized clearly.
  • Interaction: Direct manipulation of plan elements (e.g., inline editing, drag-and-drop concepts).
  • Visual Weight: Balanced to rich, allowing for more information density.
