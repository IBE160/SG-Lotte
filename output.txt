Yes, it is good practice to put double quotes around the `SUPABASE_URL` and `SUPABASE_KEY` values in your `.env` file, like this:

```
SUPABASE_URL="https://your-project-id.supabase.co"
SUPABASE_KEY="your_actual_service_role_secret_key"
```

This ensures that the values are correctly parsed as strings, especially if they contain any special characters.
